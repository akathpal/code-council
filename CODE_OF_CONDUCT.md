# Code of Conduct

code-council contributors must make participation welcoming, respectful, and safe.
Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained
disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate,
threatening, or harmful. Reports should be sent privately to the security
contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers will protect reporter
privacy as far as practical, investigate promptly, and apply consequences
proportionate to impact and recurrence.

This policy applies in repositories, issue trackers, reviews, discussions,
community events, and public representation of the project.
